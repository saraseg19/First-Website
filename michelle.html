"About Me"

Before coming to Northeastern, I was taking some classes at Truman college. I was at Truman for a total of two semesters. I decided to take some classes at Truman college first rather than jumping straight to a university because I wasn't sure if I was ready to start at a university. Before deciding on computer science I was a Finance major. I wasn't happy in the classes I was enrolled in at the time which helped me realize that Finance wasn't for me. I decided to switch my major to Computer Science because that is something that I had thought about for a while. I will admit that I was intimidated at first because I didn't know what to expect. Overall I am glad that I decided to switch majors because I am enjoying these classes more.

I decided to enroll in this course because I have some background with HTML and CSS. Back in the day I used to help my cousin make myspace layouts and tumblr themes. I enjoy more hands on work and it brings me joy. I had also taken a class at Truman where I had to build a small website sort of like this one. I enjoyed that class a lot so I knew I would enjoy this one as well.

When I am not in school I like to practice drawing and painting. I have always wanted to learn how to draw. I know that is something I can learn but will take some practice along with some dedication. In my spare time I also like listening to audio books. Listening to audio books is a great way for me to destress myself.
I also enjoy going to forest preserves and exploring them. I am a big fan of these parks because they can be interesting and definitely beautiful. A lot of these parks have great views and are perfect for when you are trying to get away from the city.

HTML Definition list

Attribute
Lets you provide more information about an element.
Meta data
Information about the document itself, such as how to present the document or what other documents are related to this one, such as style sheets.
Search engine
A program that finds web pages/sites, images, or information based on inputed criteria.
World wide web consortium (W3C)
Sets standards for HTML and provides an open, nonproprietary forum for industry and academic representatives.
Browser
Application software that allows users to access and view html documents and web pages.
