Violet

Color Information

The color violet is constantly associated with "royalty", although it isn't the only color that is linked with nobility. "Live Science" mentions that part of the reason behind this association is because rulers of several kingdoms made laws that forbid anyone from wearing violet besides the royal family (Wolchover). "99 Designs" recommends using purple for luxury items and for audiences that are mostly women (99Designs). Combining violet and red will give the impression of passion and romance; while combining violet with white and pink will give the impression of playfulness ("Color theory: a beginner's guide for designers"). In general, violet is considered a cool color because it has a higher amount of blue ("Color theory: a beginner's guide for designers").
